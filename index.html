Meme Use

WeyCoin will use a Proof-of-Meme functionality that allows online meme creators throughout the social media sphere to be essentially 'tipped' for their contribution. We aim at developing our own platform in the nearby future!

Mobile Applications

Through the use of our mobile applications, you can simply scan a meme and tip the original creator! This technology will be further developed as outlined in our

Official Roadmap

Advanced Memes

WeyCoin implements some of the most up-to-date technologies out there. We offer fully functional masternodes, extremely low transaction fees, SegWit 4x implementation, Lightning Network compatibilities and much more.

Roadmap

Welcome to our Roadmap. Dis is where we will show you our wey.

  • Q1: January - March 2018

    Wallet Integration

    In the first quarter, we aim at reaching compatibility amongst all major platforms, including: Mac OS X, Linux, Windows, iOS and Windows

  • Q1: January - March 2018

    WeyCoin to Exchanges

    In this stage of development, we have successfully achieved our goal and made it to at least one exchange (CoinHouse)! From here on we will continue expanding!

  • Q2: April - June 2018

    Proof-of-Meme

    The first mechanism of the proof of meme is set to begin development around this date. This includes QR/Meme scanning integration into our mobile apps.

  • Q4: October - December

    Development Team

    In this stage, we will begin seeking the help of other developers, as the tasks at hand to follow will require collaboration of multiple people!

  • Q1: January - March 2019

    Proof of Meme V2

    Development of our own meme-sharing platform will commence! Users will be able to like (cluck), dislike (spit), and share a meme and be rewarded directly with WAE. This will be integrated into the blockchain itself.

  • 2020 Onward

    Complete World Domination

    At this point we will have been shown da wae by The Queen and thusly bring good crops for the nation of Uganda.
